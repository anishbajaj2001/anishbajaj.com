Solar Panel

Research for MIS

As the Head of my school's Environment Club, I was assigned to take daily readings of the energy generated by the newly installed solar panels. Over a period of 8 months, I collected this data and produced a detailed report analyzing the variations in energy generations in different weather conditions.

The surplus energy generated was distributed to the Sri Aurobindo Ashram.
