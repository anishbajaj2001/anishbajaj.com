ClassifAI - iOS App for Waste Segregation

In Spring 2020, my team worked on an iOS application to draw a step closer to UC Berkeley’s Zero Waste Generation Goal by making waste segregation easier for the consumer.

We employed an image-recognition AI to detect an item in the picture and determine its appropriate disposal bin, all within 1 second.

ClassifAI is a point and shoot mobile application that uses the MobileNetV2 CoreML API along with firebase integration to maximize its speed so you don’t waste time typing and Googling how you should dispose something off. We also tried to make our design as minimal as possible so enhance user experience and navigation

The app won the award in the Best App for Social Impact category in its final showcase, and we are working on releasing it on the App Store soon
