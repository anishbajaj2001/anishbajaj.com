A-Therm

AI-Driven Automatic Thermostat

I created A-Therm during my Summer Internship at Siemens PLM Software, Gurugram, India.

The device uses its AI network to determine a suitable temperature for your AC thermostat. All you have to do is install A-Therm in the system, and the AI will handle the rest for you, saving at least 20% on electricity bills.
