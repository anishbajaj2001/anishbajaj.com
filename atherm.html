A-Therm

AI-Driven Automatic Thermostat

I created A-Therm (Automatic Thermostat) during my 6-week internship at Siemens PLM Software, Gurugram, India.

The device uses machine learning to determine the optimum temperature for an AC thermostat, reducing energy consumption by up to 30%.

A-Therm was subsequently pitched to venture capital investors at Siemens for further development, followed by its installation in the air-cooling system of the same branch.
